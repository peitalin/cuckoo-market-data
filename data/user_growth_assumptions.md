# User Growth Simulation (Year 1)

This document describes a simulated monthly active user (MAU) trajectory for a watch analytics product that evolves into a marketplace aggregator over the first 12 months.

The curve is intentionally non-linear and reflects content-driven spikes, retention compounding, and an eventual shift from pure analytics consumption toward transaction intent.
This version applies a conservative `-20%` adjustment versus the original base case, with MAU values rounded to the nearest 100.

## Assumptions Baked In

- Strong top-of-funnel via Twitter (X): analytics threads, charts, and market commentary.
- Secondary Instagram discovery funnel: visual watch content and lifestyle discovery.
- Product starts read-heavy (analytics) and later evolves toward transaction intent.
- Audience mix is enthusiasts, flippers, and dealers (high-value, low-volume market).

## High-Level Growth Shape (12 Months)

This is not linear growth. It models early breakout moments and then stabilizing, still-strong month-over-month growth.

| Month | MAU | MoM Growth | Notes |
| --- | ---: | ---: | --- |
| 1 | 200 | — | Launch, founder-led traffic |
| 2 | 600 | +200% | First Twitter threads hit |
| 3 | 1,200 | +100% | Analytics niche resonates |
| 4 | 1,800 | +50% | Early retention forming |
| 5 | 2,800 | +56% | Instagram funnel starts contributing |
| 6 | 4,000 | +43% | Repeat users increase |
| 7 | 6,000 | +50% | "Market dashboard" becomes habit |
| 8 | 8,000 | +33% | First real liquidity signals |
| 9 | 10,400 | +30% | SEO + saved dashboards |
| 10 | 13,600 | +31% | Power users emerge |
| 11 | 17,600 | +29% | Dealers onboard organically |
| 12 | 22,400 | +27% | Strong niche authority |

Healthy early-stage interpretation:
- Early spike behavior from content resonance.
- Mid/late-year stabilization in roughly a 25% to 40% MoM band.

## Content-to-MAU Mechanics

Typical Twitter (X) loop:

1. Post insight-led watch market thread (e.g., model-level price movement + chart).
2. Link to deeper dashboard and comparables on-site.
3. Capture returning visitors through saved views, alerts, and repeat analysis behavior.

Example conversion assumptions:

- 50,000 impressions.
- 2% to 4% CTR -> 1,000 to 2,000 visits.
- 20% to 30% retained -> 200 to 600 MAU added.

## Product Feel by Stage

### Months 1 to 3

Feels like a content site:
- Traffic is concentrated around specific high-performing posts.

### Months 4 to 8

Feels like a tool:
- Users return specifically to check price movement and liquidity signals.

### Months 9 to 12

Feels like a habit layer:
- "Before buying a watch, check this site" becomes user behavior.

## Stage Bands for Analytics -> Marketplace Pipeline

- 0 to 4k MAU: content-market fit.
- 4k to 12k MAU: behavior forming.
- 12k to 24k MAU: early marketplace substrate.
